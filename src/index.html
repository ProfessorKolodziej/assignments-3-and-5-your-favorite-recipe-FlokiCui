Ingredients

  • Beef Stew Meat (cubed chuck steak)
  • All-Purpose Flour (for coating and thickening)
  • Vegetables: Carrots, onions, celery, and potatoes
  • Beef Broth (for rich, savory flavor)
  • Seasonings: Garlic, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar, rosemary, thyme

How to Make Beef Stew

  1. Dice the onion and red potatoes. Slice the carrots and celery. Mince the garlic.
  2. Coat the stew meat in flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat a skillet and sear the beef until browned.
  4. Prepare the broth with Dijon, Worcestershire, soy sauce, brown sugar, rosemary, and thyme.
  5. Place everything into the slow cooker and stir well.
  6. Cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours.
  7. Stir well before serving to thicken the stew naturally.

Tips for the Best Crockpot Beef Stew

  • Sear the meat first for deep, rich flavor.
  • Add umami boosters like Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce.
  • Cook low and slow for ultimate tenderness.
  • Don't skip the veggies! They add texture and enhance the gravy.

Serving Suggestions

This stew is delicious on its own but even better with bread, polenta, rice, or egg noodles. Add a fresh veggie side to make it a complete meal.

Variations to Try

Swap out the meat, cook it in an Instant Pot, use different stocks, or add beans for extra protein.
